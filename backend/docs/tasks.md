# Tasks & Activities Management

## Tasks

Tasks are actionable items assigned to users with specific due dates and priorities.
- Associated with any CRM entity (Lead, Deal, Contact).
- Support priorities: Low, Medium, High, Urgent.
- Statuses: Pending, InProgress, Completed, Cancelled.

## Activities (Timeline)

Activities are immutable (or editable logs) of interactions.
- Automatically generated by the system (e.g., "Deal Stage Changed", "Lead Created").
- Manually added by users (e.g., "Call Log", "Meeting Notes").
- Provide a full chronological history of an entity.
