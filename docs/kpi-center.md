# KPI Center UI Architecture

The **KPI Performance Center** (`/dashboard/kpis`) presents real-time operational performance metrics across all business functions.

## Capabilities
- **Time Period Granularity**: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly views.
- **14 Core Business KPIs**:
  - Total Revenue & Monthly Revenue Growth
  - Customer Retention Rate & Churn Rate %
  - Lead Conversion Rate & Total Lead Volume
  - Average Deal Size & Average Sales Cycle Length
  - Active Workflows & Workflow Success Rate
  - AI Assistant Request Usage
  - Marketing Campaign ROI % & QR Code Scans
- **Category Filter Pills**: Filter metrics by Finance, Sales, Customers, Marketing, Operations, AI.
- **On-Demand Recalculation**: Triggers real-time re-estimation of live KPIs directly from database entities.
