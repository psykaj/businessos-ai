# AI Decision Center UI Architecture

The **AI Decision Center** (`/dashboard/insights`) surfaces high-value recommendations, operational warnings, and strategic growth opportunities identified by the AI Business Insight Engine.

## Key Features
- **Priority Categorization**: Color-coded badges for `Critical`, `High`, `Medium`, and `Low` priority items.
- **Business Impact & Recommended Action Cards**: Explains why an insight matters and provides clear next steps.
- **Interactive Resolution**: Allows business owners to take action and mark insights as resolved.
- **On-Demand AI Analysis**: Triggers live data scanning across CRM, Billing, Campaigns, and Workflows.
