# AI Copilot Settings UI

## Overview
The **AI Copilot Settings** page (`/dashboard/copilot/settings`) enables business owners to customize assistant behavior and safety controls.

## Preference Controls
1. **Business Language**: Select preferred operational language (English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi).
2. **Response Style**: Choose between Concise & Direct, Comprehensive, or Analytical.
3. **Safety & Confirmation Threshold**:
   - Destructive Actions Only (Default)
   - Always Ask Confirmation
   - Auto-Approve Low Risk Operations
4. **Proactive Insights**: Toggle automatic recommendation scanning on/off.
